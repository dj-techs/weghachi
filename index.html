— THE SIGNATURE SYSTEM

Four autonomous auditors, working in concert.

Not "if-then" automation that breaks when invoice formats shift. Graph-driven autonomous agents that query databases, weigh operational context, and navigate regulatory rules.

01

Ingestion & Verification Agent

Reads the chaos

Consumes scanned PDFs, digital field logs, and handwritten field tickets. It standardizes messy industry nomenclature — from "casing flying logistics" to "crane operations" — in a normalized matrix.

02

Operational Context & Verification Agent

Knows the truth

Securely queries drilling, completion, and logistics databases. If a vendor bills 24 hours for a pump, the Daily Drilling Report knows it logged 6 hrs of rig downtime. It flags the NPT and computes the exact tariff due.

03

Contract Compliance & Audit Agent

Enforces the rules

Cross-references invoice lines against multi-tiered Master Service Agreements (MSAs), checking every charge item for NUPRC and NCDMB statutory compliance.

04

Resolution & Escalation Agent

Closes the loop

On a variance, it compiles a discrepancy package, drafts a professional notice to the vendor or engineer, and routes it to a human supervisor's dashboard for single-click review.
